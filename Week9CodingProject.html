About

Welcome to PettingPets©! Established in 2022, by Spenser Williams, PettingPets is a business created to do what we love: pet AAALLLLL the pets!

This company began after having to make the nearly impossible decision to assist a pet of nearly 20 years across the R A I N B O W bridge. Only a few months after, another beloved pet, of some best friends of Spenser's, had to make the same transition. Seeing long-time pets, or rather, FAMILY, have to make that transition is one of the hardest things to be a part of or even witness for that matter. Because of this, Spenser wanted to start a business to spread his deep appreciation for animals, by offering to give your animals extra love. Whether they are nearing the end of their adventure in this current realm, needmore attention while their "parents" are away, or could just simply use the extra love... We would love to be the people to provide that service!
